Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log time spent outdoors?

From the Dashboard, tap the "Log Time" button. Select the duration, choose activity tags, and optionally add notes about your outdoor experience.

What is the free trial?

New users receive a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. After the trial, you can continue with a subscription to unlock premium features.

How do I set my annual goal?

During onboarding, you'll set your initial goal. You can update it anytime in Settings > Goal Settings.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. You'll be asked to confirm the deletion. This action permanently removes all your data and cannot be undone.

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings > Subscription > Restore Purchases. This will restore any active subscriptions associated with your Apple ID.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your data is stored securely and we never sell your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Manage Your Subscription

To manage, cancel, or update your subscription:

  • Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad
  • Tap your name at the top
  • Tap Subscriptions
  • Select Green Time
  • From here you can change or cancel your subscription

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. For billing questions, please contact Apple Support.
